You run upstairs to get your dog Sid. He is a Basset Hound breed. So he will need help to get down stairs.
Your eyes get blocked by an intense light coming from windows so through hearing and touch.
You reach the room Sid was barking from. You pick him up and hurry down stairs.
Those noises are making your dog scared to go outside so you open your car with your wireless key,
pick up Sid again and place him at the back seat while you start the car with rush of adrenaline.
You drive off your place and into the night. Glancing as the lights from the sky slowly fade away from your mirrors.
